- Aloittelija

- Oppilas
  - hours 1-24
  - multiple choice options are generated such that they are always at least half-correct: either hours or minutes are correct for every option
- Kisälli
  - Minutes at 5 minute intervals

    - time modified by positive or negative duration
- Mestari
